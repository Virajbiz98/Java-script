Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How does the AI CV optimization work?

Our AI analyzes your job description and compares it with your experience and skills. It then suggests optimized wording, highlights relevant skills, and creates a professional summary that matches what employers are looking for in that specific role.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. ShimmerCV processes all data in your browser - we never store your personal information on our servers. Your CV data remains on your device, and we use secure API calls to Google's Gemini for AI processing.

Are the CVs ATS-friendly?

Yes, all our templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly. We use standard formatting, appropriate section headings, and clean design to ensure your CV can be properly parsed by automated systems.

Can I create multiple versions of my CV?

With our Pro and Enterprise plans, you can create and save multiple versions of your CV. This is perfect for tailoring your application to different roles or industries.

What format will my CV be downloaded in?

Your CV will be downloaded as a high-quality PDF file, which is the industry standard format for job applications. This ensures your CV looks consistent across all devices and platforms.
